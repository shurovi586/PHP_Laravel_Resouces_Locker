Cat

Cats have sharp, retractable claws, and their teeth are adapted for stabbing, anchoring, and cutting. They almost always land on their feet when they fall from a height. Most species are nocturnal, and their eyes are adapted for seeing in low light.Cats have an adorable face with a tiny nose, a big mouth and a few whiskers under its nose. Cats are generally white but can also be brown, black, grey, cream or buff. Cats are omnivores.
