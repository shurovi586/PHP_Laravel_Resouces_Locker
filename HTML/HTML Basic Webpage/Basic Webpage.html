Fruit:

Fruit is the mature ovary of a flowering plant, typically containing seeds. Fruits come in various shapes, sizes, colors, and flavors, making them a diverse and essential part of the human diet. They are rich in vitamins, minerals, fiber, and natural sugars, providing essential nutrients and antioxidants that promote overall health. Common examples include apples, oranges, bananas, and berries. Fruits can be eaten fresh, dried, or processed into juices, jams, and other products. They are often enjoyed as snacks, desserts, or ingredients in various dishes.
